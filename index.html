How it's monitored

A dual-layer monitoring system

One layer watches from the outside on a schedule; one layer watches what real visitors actually experience. Together they cross-validate, so an issue rarely slips through both.

Checkly Synthetic
Scheduled checks

Runs scripted checks on a timer to confirm key pages, forms and CTAs work — even when no one is looking.

  • Forms submit and leads flow into HubSpot (freshness-based)
  • Paid landing pages load; conversion pixels fire
  • Alerts the moment a check fails
Sentry Real user
Real-user monitoring

Watches what actual visitors hit — errors, broken interactions, slow submissions and Core Web Vitals in the moment.

  • Errors and broken form interactions from real sessions
  • Core Web Vitals & Apdex from live traffic
  • Session replay to see exactly what broke
Ahrefs SEO & links
Site-wide crawl

Crawls the whole site for link and SEO health — now feeding Link Integrity and SEO Health live.

  • Broken internal & external links, redirect chains, 404s
  • Broken images and missing assets
  • SEO health signals across audited pages
